Journeys to Cloth, 2022

Interactive installation to engage end-users as participants in the creative design process.

Journeys, 2021

During lockdown in the UK, participants here and in Europe, were asked to send images of landscapes that moved them - through colour, composition or texture. These images were collected and processed with code to reveal shifting gradients of colour leaving traces of memory behind, and transforming into new fields of vision. The video was made with processing.
